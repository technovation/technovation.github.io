  <ol>
    <li>
      Role &#8211; As described by the team leader in list of open positions.
    </li>
    <li>
      Selection <ol>
        <li>
          Eligibility Criteria <ol>
            <li>
              Any student of IIT Bombay who has completed at least 1 semester
            </li>
          </ol>
        </li>
        
        <li>
          Judging Criteria <ol>
            <li>
              Passion for the project
            </li>
            <li>
              Adequate of technical skills for the particular post
            </li>
            <li>
              Past experience
            </li>
          </ol>
        </li>
        
        <li>
          Selection Procedure <ol>
            <li>
              The student has to apply for a particular post in the list of open positions for a project.
            </li>
            <li>
              He shall be interviewed by Technovation manager and team leader and chosen for the post if he is the best choice
            </li>
          </ol>
        </li>
      </ol>
    </li>
    
    <li>
      Removal <ol>
        <li>
          Criteria &#8211; Null contribution towards project and/or frequent disobedience
        </li>
        <li>
          Procedure - <ol>
            <li>
              The team leader proposes removal of a team member
            </li>
            <li>
              If two-thirds majority if the team vote against the member he is removed from the team.
            </li>
          </ol>
        </li>
      </ol>
    </li>
  </ol>
</ol>

      Monthly Review Meetings (MRM) <ol>
        <li>
          Purpose &#8211; A periodic review of the progress of a team, brainstorming and evaluation of progress.
        </li>
        <li>
          Each team shall be given 10 minutes to present their progress with and 5 minutes for interaction with audience
        </li>
        <li>
          Attendance by all team leaders and team members is mandatory.
        </li>
        <li>
          Presentation Contents <ol>
            <li>
              Progress since last review meeting
            </li>
            <li>
              Demonstration of whatever tangible has been achieved (prototype, model under construction, working model)
            </li>
            <li>
              Costs incurred and corresponding bills
            </li>
            <li>
              Setting target for the next 15 days in correspondence to next goal on the timeline
            </li>
          </ol>
        </li>
        
        <li>
          Bills for that month would be reimbursed subject to completion of documentation of whatever was presented.
        </li>
      </ol>
    </li>
    
    <li>
      Documentation <ol>
        <li>
          A Project management system will be set up and teams are required to upload documentation as soon as any work is done.
        </li>
        <li>
          It shall be reviewed every 15 days, esp written and pictorial description.
        </li>
      </ol>
    </li>
  </ol>
</ol>
